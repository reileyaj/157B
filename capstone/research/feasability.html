Switching Between Speculative Designs

Since I am focusing my project around speculative design, I want to explore different paths that car/pedestrian-centric infrastructure and vehicle design can take. The ideas themselves will be built in rendered with three.js, but I want users to be able to smoothly switch between these designs at any time. I may be able to do this with css animations, but since those are tricky and time-consuming for me, I may employ the help of anime.js.
