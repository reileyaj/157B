Large Cars

Personal vehicles have been getting larger and larger in the U.S. A subset of these vehicles, classified as light trucks (which includes pickup trucks and SUVs), have different safety regulations under U.S. law. These vehicles tend to be getting larger, which reduces driver visibility, and taller, which, in a collision with a pedestrian, is more likely to push them under the vehicle rather than onto the hood of the vehicle. This is much more dangerous for the pedestrian.
